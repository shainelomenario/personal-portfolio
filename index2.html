Project 1: Spotify ⤏ Learn More

Worked with my peers on a group assignment on Spotify. Our objective was to see how much users are on Spotify by sending out surveys and interviewing some of the people on their thoughts on Spotify premium if they will purchase it or just continue with the free plan. The respondents gave suggestions on how Spotify can improve and what features it excels at.
